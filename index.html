Donald Trump's Goals After Inauguration

Explore the key goals that President Donald Trump aims to achieve after his inauguration, along with potential challenges that might arise.

  1. Cut Taxes

    Why This Could Backfire:

    Tax cuts may not benefit all Americans equally and could lead to an escalation of national debt.

  2. Increase Military Spending

    Why This Might Lead to Global Tension:

    Increased military spending could be seen as a provocation by other nations and may result in an arms race.

  3. Immigration Reform

    Why This Could Divide the Country:

    Proposed reforms may alienate certain voter groups and lead to social divisions.

  4. Repeal Obamacare

    Why This Might Be Risky:

    Repealing Obamacare could lead to millions losing their health coverage and create an unstable healthcare environment.
