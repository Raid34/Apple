Donald Trump's Plans for America

From ending inflation to keeping TikTok, here are five things Donald Trump will do after his inauguration.

  1. Ending Inflation:

    Donald Trump plans to address inflation by implementing stricter fiscal policies, reducing government spending, and focusing on deregulating industries to boost economic growth. He aims to stabilize prices and improve purchasing power for Americans.

  2. Adding More Fossil Fuel Emissions:

    The former president is expected to roll back environmental regulations, opening up more opportunities for oil, coal, and natural gas production. He argues this will create jobs and reduce dependency on foreign energy sources.

  3. Pushing the TikTok Ban Back by 90 Days:

    Trump's administration plans to revisit the TikTok ban, granting the company a 90-day extension to address national security concerns while negotiating potential ownership changes.

  4. Starting a Meme Coin:

    Trump reportedly intends to introduce a cryptocurrency branded as a "Meme Coin" to appeal to younger voters and capitalize on the growing interest in digital assets.

  5. Deporting Undocumented Migrants:

    He has pledged to reinstate stricter immigration policies, prioritizing the deportation of undocumented migrants and increasing border security measures.
